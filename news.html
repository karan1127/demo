Q: Can I get delivery today?

A: Usually it is not possible, you have to order a day in advance, before 6pm. If you are near to the factory kitchen, then you can order on the same day in the morning and pick up from our Okhla kitchen later that day.

Q: What time will my order be delivered?

A: It depends on the area of town you live as we have a delivery route that we maintain. If you have a special delivery timing request, you can inquire, but we may not be able to get it to you, and you may need to take your order the day before if it is for early morning. We do not do evening or midnight deliveries.

Q: Do you charge for delivery?

A: Delivery is free, but you must order according to our delivery minimum. It is Rs 800 minimum for deliver, Rs 1000 for Vadodara City. If some areas are harder to reach then the minimum charge is higher.. If you are ordering under our delivery minimum, you can still place your order and pick up from our factory.

Q: Do you do gluten free or sugar less items?

A: We do gluten free brownies, but otherwise no, we currently do not offer either gluten free or sugarless items.

Q: What items are egg-less on your menu?

A: Following are the egg-less items:

  • -All breads, bagels and bagel chips
  • -Shortbread Cookies
  • -Chocolate Ginger Cookies
  • -Eggless Chocolate Muffin
  • -Chocolate, white chocolate chunk loaf cake
  • -Florentine Cookies
  • -Granola Bars
  • -Certain birthday cakes as listed below.

Q: What cakes are available egg-less?

A: Double chocolate, After 8, Chocolate Truffle, Black Forest, Simply Vanilla, Fresh Fruit. Not an egg less option- Cheesecake, Peanut Butter Brownie, Red Velvet,Carrot Cake

Q: Photo Cakes- What is it? How does it work? Where do I send the photo?

A: These are all the details about photo cake:

  • -It is printed on paper made of corn flour and rice flour and other fully edible ingredients.
  • -It is printed on a printer with food color ‘ink’, you get an exact image like you are printing from your own computer printer.
  • -Send your personal image to our email, redmoonbakery@gmail.com or give us a call or email us and let us know what image you want, something we could find online ourselves, like a cartoon character etc.
  • -And let us know if you also want a message printed on the cake as well.
  • -Order at least a day in advance in the morning.

Q: Is Red Moon Bakery a chain?

A:We are an Indian Private Limited company started and owned and operated by the Hamblys, Canadian bakers that have made Vadodara their home.

About Us

The Red Moon Bakery is a Canadian Bakery in South Delhi. It opened it's doors in December 2007. It is owned and operated by David and Anna Hambly from Canada. You can find David and Anna mostly every day baking up a storm in our Okhla Kitchen. We are foodies at heart and are happy to be making New Delhi our second home! We have a fun loving and loyal staff that are here to serve you, so give us a call sometime soon!
